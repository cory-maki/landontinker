Get to Know Him

A Builder in Training, On and Off the Jobsite

Landon Tinker is finishing a construction science degree at Texas A&M University, where classroom coursework meets real jobsite experience. Between internships at a national homebuilder and a residential construction firm, Landon has spent his college years learning the industry from the ground up.

Long before his first internship, though, Landon was already building. For seven years running, he has volunteered every November with his family, framing and finishing homes for families in Costa Rica through Youth With A Mission's Homes of Hope program.

That combination of formal training and volunteer service shapes how Landon approaches every project. Read Landon's full story to see how it all fits together.
